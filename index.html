About the Show

Creator: Jean-Yves Raimbaud

Genre: Comedy / Slapstick / Animation

Origin: France

First Aired: January 6, 1998

Fun Fact: The show barely uses dialogue-its humor is global, making it one of the most
loved cartoons worldwide.

In each episode, Oggy's house becomes a battleground of wits and chaos, packed with explosions, traps, and food fights.
It's a symbol of endless rivalry mixed with heartwarming friendship (sometimes).

Meet the Characters

Oggy the cat:

A lazy, lovable cat who wants nothing but peace, food, and TV. He's the definition of chill until the cockroaches strike.

Joey :

The leader of the cockroach trio, known for his cunning plans to annoy Oggy and steal his food.

Dee Dee :

Orange and obsessed with eating everything‚Äîfrom food to soap to metal!

Marky :

Tall, green, and laid-back. He's the cool troublemaker who enjoys watching chaos unfold.

Jack :

Oggy's cousin and opposite. Short-tempered, tough, and always ready to blow up the house fighting the roaches.

Bob :

The friendly neighborhood dog who often gets caught in the crossfire between Oggy and the cockroaches.

Olivia :

Oggy's love interest. Sweet, caring, and often the voice of reason amidst the chaos.
